About the Creator

Hi! I'm Fox! I like to doodle but I'm not very good at it. That's why I made all these little creatures, because they're really easy shapes to draw! For digital art I used to use Krita and I highly recommend it, but ever since getting an iPad I've really enjoyed using Procreate. Of course, nothing can beat good old pen and paper sometimes.

Some other things I really love are fountain pens, baking, and learning about outer space. I also really enjoy wearing hats of all types. I think my ultimate goal in life is to just vibe, and to help and cheer up whoever I can while I'm at it. Hopefully this website cheered you up a little! Life can be rough sometimes, but there are so many fun and pretty things in the world to marvel over. Find joy in the little things, ya know?

Anyways, below are some of the other things I've drawn. Hope you find them interesting, and thanks for stopping by!
