Moop are small deer-like creatures. They gained their name because they make "moop" noises of varying tones and intensities in order to communicate. Moop are extremely friendly creatures with no known enemies or predators. It is thought that they are so kind and innocent that no one wishes to hurt them.

Moop have short and thick reddish-brown fur, which is waterproof and helps to keep them warm in the winter. They are native to temperate forests and grasslands, but can be found all over the world due to their affinity for the Mysterious Men, whose travels are not limited to any particular biome.

Moop reproduce sexually, although all Moop are hermaphrodites and posess the ability to bear a child and inseminate a partner. Moop achieve their full size at around 8 months, and leave their parents after a year. Moop are relatively ambivalent towards members of their own species, and only meet with partners to engage in intercourse. Despite their friendly disinterest in members of their own species, Moop have an odd affinity for Mysterious Men and will often join them on their travels.

Moop are very sturdy creatures. They do not get sick or injured easily, and they can carry up to 3 times their own body weight on their backs. They are not very fast, but they are capable of walking extremely long distances and surviving on very little sleep.
